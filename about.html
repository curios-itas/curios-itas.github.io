About Us

curiositas is a revolutionary new science show looking to compete with the likes of Bill Nye the Science Guy. This show is mainly targeted at elementary to junior high students, however high school students may also enjoy the show.
This show came into existance we, a group of friends, found out that Bill Nye's show was discontinued. We wanted to create a new show that could educate and entertain children in school and at home.
Our mission is to create educational videos for children and schools. No matter where you are, you can enjoy an entertaining, quality, and reliable science lesson.
